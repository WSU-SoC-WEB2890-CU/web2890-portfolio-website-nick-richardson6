BookHive

Project Details

BookHive is a reading tracker and community platform that helps users log their books, track their reading progress, and discover new titles. The project was created as part of a UX design course, with the goal of building a more intuitive and focused alternative to cluttered book tracking apps. My role included UX research, interface design, and prototyping in Figma.

Scope

The project focused on simplifying core actions like adding and reviewing books. I wanted the interface to feel calm, clear, and easy to navigate. The target users were avid readers who value a simple way to keep track of their reading habits.

Design Decisions

Results

The final prototype gave users a smooth and focused reading experience. Testers described it as easy to navigate and visually clean. This project helped me strengthen my ability to design with real user needs in mind.
