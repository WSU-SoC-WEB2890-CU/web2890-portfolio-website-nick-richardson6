About Me

Hi, I’m Nick Richardson. I am a Web & UX student with a passion for good design and creating products that people enjoy using. I care deeply about building experiences that feel intuitive, thoughtful, and accessible to everyone.

I love working closely with users to solve problems and make sense of complex ideas. Whether it’s through research, testing, or refining interfaces for accessibility, I’m most excited when design decisions are driven by real user needs.

Outside of design, I work full time for a consumer research company, and in my free time I enjoy golfing, training in jiu-jitsu, and hiking. These hobbies keep me grounded and remind me that great design—like great movement—is about balance, flow, and attention to detail.
